Fayose Wins Ekiti

Author: Emeka Okoye
Topics: Election , Elections in Nigeria , Ekiti State , Kayode Fayemi , Ayo Fayose , PDP , LP , APC

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) candidate has been elected Governor of Ekiti State, after winning the gubernatorial elections that took place on June 21, 2014. Fayose polled 203,090 votes, beating the incumbent Governor Kayode Fayemi who could only gather 120,433 votes.

The Returning Officer for the 2014 governorship elections and Vice Chancellor of the Federal University of Oye, Ekiti, Professor Isaac Azuzu, announced the results early this morning, June 22, 2014.

Fayose, was governor of Ekiti State from May 29, 2003 to October 16, 2006.
